Trying to slow down

Mood: tired

My to-do list never ends and I keep saying yes to everything. I am worried that if I rest, things will fall apart, but I am also exhausted all the time.

Reply

It is okay to rest before everything is finished. You are allowed to be a person before you are productive.

New city, new loneliness

Mood: lonely

I moved for school and everyone else seems to have found their group already. I talk to people in class, but it still feels like I am on the outside.

Reply

Making real friends takes longer than social media makes it seem. You are not late, you are just human.

Afraid of disappointing people

Mood: anxious

I am scared to set boundaries because I do not want people to think I am selfish. Even small decisions make me nervous.

Reply

Saying no is not selfish, it is a way of being honest. People who care about you will want you to feel safe and comfortable.
